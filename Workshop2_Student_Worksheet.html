Rate Expression: Propane Combustion

8 pts

C3H8(g) + 5O2(g) → 3CO2(g) + 4H2O(g)

Walkthrough Hint

Imagine a factory where different things are consumed and produced at different speeds, but they’re all linked by the recipe (the balanced equation).
The rate expression divides each species’ rate by its stoichiometric coefficient so every term gives the same overall reaction rate.
Rules: Coefficient goes in the denominator • Reactants get a minus sign • Products stay positive.

The Rule
For   aA + bB → cC + dD:
Rate = −1aΔ[A]Δt = −1bΔ[B]Δt = +1cΔ[C]Δt = +1dΔ[D]Δt

Write the complete rate expression for this reaction. Fill in the blanks:

Rate = Δ[C3H8]Δt = 1 Δ[O2]Δt = 1 Δ[CO2]Δt = 1 Δ[H2O]Δt
